POLYHEDRA

Collect. Upgrade. Dominate.

How to Play

  • Everyone starts with a D4 (protected from loss until your first choice)
  • Each round: pick a die, roll it N times (N = sides). Highest total wins
  • Winner chooses: Duplicate your die OR Upgrade to the next die
  • Losers lose a random die to the pool
  • First to reach the target number of D100s wins everything!
D4 → D6 → D8 → D10 → D12 → D20 → D100
